Why Choose Gene-ID

About Gene-ID

Gene ID is a pioneering company that revolutionizes gene expression through its user-friendly website. With a strong focus on efficiently converting DNA into RNA and protein, Gene ID bridges the gap between computer science and biology, providing an accessible and engaging playground for pre-med majors, researchers, and bio-informatics professionals. By offering a seamless and intuitive user experience, Gene ID simplifies the complex process of gene expression, making it easy for users to explore the fascinating world of genetics and accelerate their scientific endeavors.

Our Vision

Our Vision is simply to create an easy to use tool for pre-meds

Our Mission

Our Mission is to get 4000 users by the end of this year and make sure that at least 10 biology professors are using this tool in their classes.
